Return and Exchange
Return Policy
1. We accept exchange or return within 30 days from the date you received the order.
2. Original receipt must be presented for exchange or return.
3. Original product is required. Exchanged or returned product(s) must be in its new and original condition, original product packaging attached with its original price tags and labels.
4. Innerwear and packaged items cannot be exchanged or returned due to our strict hygiene policy.
5. Product(s) can be changed to available sizes only if the product is not used. Color of products are not able to change.
6. Product(s) purchased outside of Singapore is not eligible for exchange or return.
7. Product(s) sold at discounted price are not allowed to be exchanged.
8. We can reject any requst to return, exhange and refund of any item.
